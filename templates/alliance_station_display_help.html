Alliance Station Status Color Legend

A-Stop (Driver Station A-Stopped): a-stop

This status indicates that a driver station's A-Stop is tripped.

E-Stop (Emergency Stop): e-stop

This status indicates that a driver station's E-Stop is tripped.

Bypass Mode: bypass

This status indicates that a driver station is in bypass mode.

Team Disconnected: disconnected

This status indicates that a driver station is disconnected.

Team Connected: connected

This status indicates that a driver station is connected.

Count: count

This status indicates that official scorers are counting game pieces, and the field should not be opened.

Field Reset: reset

This status indicates that the field reset crew should begin clearing the field.
